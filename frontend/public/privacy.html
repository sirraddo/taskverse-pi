Privacy Policy

TaskVerse Earn | Effective date: June 16, 2026

What We Collect

When you log in via Pi Browser, we receive your Pi username and Pi UID from the Pi Network. We store these to manage your task submissions and rewards.

How We Use Your Data

Your Pi username and UID are used solely to identify you within TaskVerse Earn, track your task completions, and process Pi payments to your wallet.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Payment data is processed through the Pi Network platform.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on cloud infrastructure. We retain data for as long as your account is active.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us. Deleting your account removes your username, submissions, and earnings history.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via the TaskVerse Earn app in Pi Browser at taskverse-pi.vercel.app.
